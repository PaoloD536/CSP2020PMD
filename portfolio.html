Light Bot



Project 1.1.9 "Lift off"


A spaceship gets stuck in space and doesn't know where to go. The ship changes directions by spinning in circles and turning left and right. Once the ship found out where to go, it flew in that direction off screen.

Project 1.2.5 "Snake Game"

A hungry snake travels around in the ocean and trys to eat as many pinapples as possible. The snake can only travel so far and if it touches the border of its hunting grounds, it gets killed and is reborn to repeat the same task.

Project 1.3.1 "Dodgeball"

You navigate your little stickman around a rectangular court and try avoiding 3 massive dodgeballs. The goal is to see how long you can last without loosing all 3 of your lives. If you touch a dodgeball, then you loose one life.

Project "Rock Paper Scissors" (Scratch Program)

This is Rock Paper Scissors but in Scratch (Link to Game ---> https://scratch.mit.edu/projects/462038487/). You can choose between rock, paper, or scissors with r, s, and p. The opponent is randomized so results can be either win, lose, or tie.
